We use debt-to-income (DTI) to show how comfortable a monthly payment is for you.

DTI rules

DTI = (your existing debts + new mortgage payment) ÷ your monthly income.

  • Under 30%Safe
  • 30–40%Moderate
  • 40–50%Risky
  • Over 50%Not recommended
